# Pinnacle Tubs — Launch Checklist

Tasks to complete before calling the site officially launched. Check each off
with the owner. Items marked "later" should wait until the final domain is
decided, to avoid redoing SEO work.

## Owner sign-off

- [ ] Owner approves the overall design and page flow
- [ ] Owner approves all copy/wording
- [ ] Owner approves all photos and videos
- [ ] Owner approves the contact information shown

## Business details (confirm exact, final values)

- [ ] Business name (legal + display)
- [ ] Phone number
- [ ] Email address
- [ ] Street address, city, state, ZIP
- [ ] Instagram handle / URL
- [ ] Business hours — decide whether to show hours on the site; if yes,
      provide the exact hours (the old site did not list any)

## Domain

- [ ] Decide the final domain (e.g. pinnacletubs.com)
- [ ] Connect the domain to Vercel and confirm HTTPS works
- [ ] Confirm the site loads on both `www` and non-`www`

## SEO and search visibility (do AFTER the final domain is connected)

- [ ] Add page metadata for the final domain (title, description, canonical)
- [ ] Add social preview image + Open Graph / Twitter tags
- [ ] Add `sitemap` and `robots`
- [ ] Add LocalBusiness JSON-LD (name, address, phone, geo, hours)
- [ ] Set up Google Search Console and submit the sitemap
- [ ] Update the Google Business Profile website link to the new domain

## Pre-launch testing

- [ ] Click every nav item: logo, Home, Tubs, Contact all scroll correctly
- [ ] Test the "See Our New Models" CalSpa button (opens calspas.com)
- [ ] Test the Contact buttons: Call, Email, Get directions, Instagram
- [ ] Confirm all photos and videos load (no broken images)
- [ ] Check on a phone (iPhone + Android) and on desktop
- [ ] Confirm no sideways scrolling on mobile
- [ ] Confirm scroll/video sections play smoothly

## Billing / operations

- [ ] Keep the Vercel account active and billing current
- [ ] Keep the domain registration active and billing current
- [ ] Note where login credentials for Vercel, GitHub, and the domain are stored

## Notes

- Rollback instructions, asset rules, and how to edit content safely are in
  `OWNER-HANDOFF.md`.
- The approved sections are locked; see `CLAUDE.md` for the hard boundary.
